Week One

Reflection

Something you should know about me is for short term goals (a daily/weekly/monthly goal) I plan them out to the T. But for long term goals I prefer going with the flow and seeing what I learn and where life takes me. So for this course, for weekly assignments I want to be able to create things that I will be able to impress my most unimpressed friends with. For the overall long term goal of this project I want to see what I learn throughout the semester and see what the outcome of all those little things will be when put together.

An idea I would like to explore futher would be creating something that has both a physical and non-physical elements. Like how would I be able to create an experience where you could interact with something in real life but then it can create a reaction on a screen or vice versa. That would be cool.

Biggest fear?

Falling behind...

There's so much content in this course that I'm afraid of losing control and missing work and being left behind. Because catching up is probably one of the hardest things to do. - I say this as I write this assignment 2 weeks late :)
