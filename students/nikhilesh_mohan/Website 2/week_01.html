Week One

Discussion

With the world facing new problems and new opportunities everyday, if not every minute,
a good question to ask oursleves would be are we ready for it ? In my humble opinion we are
still miles away from unlocking our true potential which makes me think are we ready to accept
what tomorrow has to offer for us. For example it is very obvious that there is a feud with the
inclusion of Artificial Intelligence in various fields and very few people like Elon Musk and
Jeff Bezoz constantly try to pioneer in the field despite the hurdles, but if we go back in time
a bit there were people who shuned the smartphone away, today can we even think of a second
without them around ? It is this idea of tackling the people's mentality for the greater good
which will be key in taking us to humanity's next step.

The first step we can take in that direction is closing the gap between man and machine.
A powerful synergy between biology and technology can unlock a variety of unforeseen possibilities
and it may start with how we interact with our machines. Currently we treat machines as a heap of
things put together to complete a task. Our perspective towards these machines are completely
devoid of character and emotion. If we can shift our perspective and imagine a scenario where
the machine connected with us and suggested a better way of doing something and we had an
optimistic approach to that, how much more productive could we be. It also important to analyze the
problems that AI might bring with it such as bias based on the data it is given, the difficulty of
integrating empathy and its ability of only making rational decisions often neglecting human factors.

I will leave you with a line Thanos mentions in Endgame,
"As long as there are those that remember what was, there will always be those that are unable to accept what can be."
