Nikhilesh Mohan

only known as Nik, Nikhi or Nikhil is a visionary whose passion
is to create and innovate for a better today. Currently in his
2nd year of design school, he is pursuing a dual concentration
degree in Product and multimedia design hoping to bridge the
gap between man and machine.

His ethos is driven by the desire to seek and learn anything and
everything possible to apply design in an array of mediums and
see how they coexist. One of his peculiar interests is in Artificial
Intelligence and how it can be made into just A.nother I.diot that
can enhance our day to day life.

An immense fascination in stories, photography and technology
put together influences his rationally crazy ideas and the drive
to make them a reality. One such crazy goal, if not the most
ambitious one is to impact the lives of a billion people through
design.
