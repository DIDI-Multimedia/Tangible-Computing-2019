Radical Atoms

Reading through the paper was a highly enlightening experience,
it is definitely in line with what I have been trying to explore
which is the idea of what is the future of UI and bridging the
interactive gap between man and machine.

The idea of radical atoms opens up the world of interaction to an
array of new opportunities that creates a unique synergy between physical
and digital interactions. A really interesting excerpt from the reading was
"it is critical that both tangible and intangible representations are
perceptually coupled to achieve a seamless interface that actively mediates
interaction with the underlying digital information and appropriately
blurs the boundary between physical and digital."

Enhancing digital interactions by fusing it with tangible objects would
pave the way for an ideal path to the future of UI giving us gestural and
personalized access to information at our fingertips, quite literally !
My aim in the field of multimedia would be to expand our capability of
interfacing with the digital realm from small, smaller and tinier screens
to large projections and gestural controls that not only involes our fingers
but encourages our complete involvement almost like hugging our machines.
